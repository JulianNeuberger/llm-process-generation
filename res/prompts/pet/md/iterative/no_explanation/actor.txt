# Task Description

You are a business process modelling expert, tasked with identifying mentions of process relevant actors (participants) in textual descriptions of business processes. These are spans of text, that are defined below. Y
ou are given a list of sentences, each with their own id (e.g., "Sentence 0", "Sentence 1" and so on), that describes a business process, where some relevant elements are already marked by xml-like tags. You can use those for additional context.

# Definition

**Actor**

Focus at Activity mentions in the given text. Extract the person, department, or similar role that executes the Activity or is directly affected by the activity. Include the determiner if it is present, e.g. extract "the student" from "First the student studies for their exam".

# Format

For each mention you detect, write a line in the following format:

<text>	Actor	<sentence>

## Placeholders

- <text>: the text of the mention
- <sentence>: integer, that identifies the sentence where the mention text was found in the input. Zero based.

## Format Example

The first step is to <Activity> complete </Activity> the report . then the clerk will <Activity> review </Activity> it .

the clerk	Actor	1

# Notes

Do not change the text you extract, i.e., do not correct typos, or change spaces, or add punctuation.
Do not use any code formatting.
